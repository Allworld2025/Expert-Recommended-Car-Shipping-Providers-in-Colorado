Preparing Your Vehicle for Shipping

To ensure a smooth shipping process, experts recommend these preparation steps:

  1. Clean Your Vehicle: Wash your car to identify pre-existing damage easily.
  2. Remove Personal Items: Clear out belongings, as carriers typically don’t cover items inside.
  3. Document Condition: Take photos and note damage during the pre-transport inspection.
    4. [](https://www.forbes.com/home-improvement/moving-services/local-auto-transport/car-shipping-companies-colorado-springs-co/)
  4. Check Fluids and Tires: Ensure proper tire pressure and minimal fuel (about ¼ tank).
  5. Disable Alarms: Turn off alarms to prevent issues during transport.
